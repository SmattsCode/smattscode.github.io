# Tuple
A single row of data from a table.

Example:

| Title               | Series      | Author               |
| ------------------- | ----------- | -------------------- |
| ==Mr Greedy==       | ==Mr Men==  | ==Roger Hargreaves== |
| Little Miss Naughty | Little Miss | Roger Hargreaves     |

From the table above the highlighted row containing the cells with `Mr Greedy`, `Mr Men`, and `Roger Hargreaves` as their contents is a Tuple.
